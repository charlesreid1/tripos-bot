If $yz = 1$ and $y_{r} = (1/r!) D_{x}^{r}y$, $z_{s} = (1/s!) D_{x}^{s}z$, then
\[
\frac{1}{z^{3}}
\begin{matrix}
    z    & z_{1}& z_{2}\\
    z_{1}& z_{2}& z_{3}\\
    z_{2}& z_{3}& z_{4}
\end{matrix}
= \frac{1}{y^{2}}
\begin{matrix}
    y_{2}& y_{3}\\
    y_{3}& y_{4}
\end{matrix}.
\]
