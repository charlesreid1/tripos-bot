If $(a - b^{3})b > 0$, then
\[
\bigsqrtb[3]{a + \frac{9b^{3} + a}{3b}\bigsqrtp{\frac{a - b^{3}}{3b}}} +
\bigsqrtb[3]{a - \frac{9b^{3} + a}{3b}\bigsqrtp{\frac{a - b^{3}}{3b}}}
\]
is rational. (Each of the numbers under a cube root is of the form
\[
\left\{\alpha + \beta\bigsqrtp{\frac{a - b^{3}}{3b}}\right\}^{3}
\]
where $\alpha$~and~$\beta$ are rational.)
