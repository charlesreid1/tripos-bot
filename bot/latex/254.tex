Prove that
\begin{align*}
D_{x}^{n} \{(\cos x)/x\}
  &= \{P_{n} \cos(x + \tfrac{1}{2}n\pi)
     + Q_{n} \sin(x + \tfrac{1}{2}n\pi)\}/x^{n+1},\\
D_{x}^{n} \{(\sin x)/x\}
  &= \{P_{n} \sin(x + \tfrac{1}{2}n\pi)
     - Q_{n} \cos(x + \tfrac{1}{2}n\pi)\}/x^{n+1},
\end{align*}
where $P_{n}$ and~$Q_{n}$ are polynomials in~$x$ of degree $n$~and~$n-1$ respectively.
